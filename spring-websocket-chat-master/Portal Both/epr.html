This is used to capture information from patients throughout the care pathway, regardless of where the services are delivered.

How to use Electronic Patient Record module

You can learn "How to use Electronic Patient Record module" here.

Workflow for Electronic Patient Record
 

Login with username and password
Select Patient Module from home screen
Search and select a Patient
If the patient is already referred into the service you will be required to select a contact type to access the patient record.If patient is not currently referred into the service you will need to add a referal to the patient.
Click on Assessment section from left pane, then select the required assessment from the drop and then click on Show button.
It will redirect you to the assessments page, click on start button to view all the subcategories.
Go through each and every section of assessment by selecting the answers and extra details for every answer you select and click on next button
It will then give you option to view details entered, click on the hyperlink which will then display all the details added
Click on end button after confirming the details
It will then display all the links. Click on save button to save any prescription or request that were made during the assessments which can ve viewed and printed.
It will display assessment details and will give you option to view or print the assessment in pdf format
Go back to the Contact history page by clicking on Contact history section
You can now view all the patients medical history and details added from the assessment or from any other section

  2. How do you prescribe a drug from an assessment?
    A) Go to the medication section of the assessment > click on the desired drug listed on the buttons below > Fill out the prescription at the right side of the screen (does, frequency, route etc.) > click save
    B) Click assessment category > click +assessment > enter info > click save
    C) Click on the medication section > click upload document button > choose file from your computer > click save
    D) Click on the view dropdown at the top > click on prescription > choose your desired prescription > click edit > view the preview > click send for approval/approved
  3. What are the steps to doing an assessment?
    A) Click assessment category > click +assessment > enter info > click save
    B) Click on the assessment category on the left > click start > enter info based on the assessment > click next for next section of the assessment > repeat steps > click end > view summary > click save
    C) Fill out the assessment form physically > click the +assessment at top > click upload document button > choose file from your computer > click save
    D) Any of the above is correct
  4. How are the steps to doing a lab request in an assessment?
