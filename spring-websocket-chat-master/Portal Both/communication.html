Communication

Cellma offers a unique function to share information via a patient portal, patient app, text or email. These can be used to communicate with the patient for appointment reminders, results, treatment options and repeat prescriptions. All conversations are automatically recorded within the patient record within Cellma.


Communication

Cellma offers a unique function to share information via a patient portal, patient app, text or email. These can be used to communicate with the patient for appointment reminders, results, treatment options and repeat prescriptions. All conversations are automatically recorded within the patient record within Cellma.

How to use Communication module

You can learn "How to use Communication module" here.

Workflow for Communication
 

Login with username and password
Go to Communication module
Search and Select Patient
Send Patient Communication to patient via Text or email
Search list of Text/Emails Sent to patient and users
Add/Edit Messaging group
Add Conversation
Add 1 user and 2 patients in conversation
Remove Participants(1 Patient) from conversation
Select the conversation to start sending messages
Take a photo and send
Assign chat to other user
Close conversation
Logout

Questions

  1. In Cellma, how is consent given for patients to receive their appointments and billing information via email or text message?
    A) Log into Cellma > go to the communications module > click the consent icon > check “yes” or “no” in accordance to patients approval
    B) Log into Cellma > Create a patient > check the “send email/text” box after duplicate check screen
    C) Select “yes” or “no” on the communication consent pop up after creating an appointment, a bill, lab request or any of that sort in accordance with the patients’ approval
    D) Any of the above is correct
  2. How do you add a chat with a user on Cellma?
    A) Log into Cellma > on the left side of your home screen click the chat icon > Click add chat icon > Click “user” radio button > Enter username > click search > check the box for all the participants you wish to add > click add
    B) Log into Cellma > go to admin > click on add chat > search for another admin user > click the link > start chat
    C) Go to the communications module > click on start chat > send link to your phone > Tap on link to open chat
    D) Any of the above is correct
  3. How do you add a chat with a patient on Cellma?
    A) Log into Cellma > on the left side of your home screen click the chat icon > Click add chat icon > Click “patient” radio button > Enter patient barcode, given name, family name > click search > check the box for all the participants you wish to add > click add
    B) Log into Cellma > go to admin > click on add chat > search for another admin user > click the link > start chat
    C) Go to the communications module > click on start chat > send link to your phone > Tap on link to open chat
    D) Any of the above is correct
  4. How do you search for a conversation?
    A) Click on the link sent by the Cellma > open in-app on your cellular device > enter the name of the conversation in the search bar > click search
    B) Click on the chat icon on the left of your Cellma home screen > type the name of the conversation in the free text box > the type of conversation > click search
    C) Go to the communications module > click on start chat > send link to your phone > Tap on link to open chat
    D) Go to the admin module > click on chats > search chats
  5. How do you assign a chat in Cellma?
    A) This is not applicable in Cellma
    B) Go to the admin module > click on chats > click on assign > search user > click the add button
    C) Click on the chats icon on the left of the home screen > Click the assign chats icon > check the boxes of the desired chats to be assigned > search for the user > Check the box to add the user > click the assign button
    D) Any of the above is applicable
  6. How do you create a messaging group on Cellma?
    A) This is not applicable in Cellma
    B) Go to the admin module > click on chats > click on group > click the add button
    C) Click on the chats icon on the left of your home screen > click on the messaging group icon above > Type the name of the group you desire > click on the add group button
    D) Any of the above is applicable
  7. What are the three types of chat participants there are in Cellma?
    A) Admin, standard, super
    B) Admin, patient, doctor
    C) Nurses, doctor, patient
    D) All, user patient
  8. How do you take an image in a conversation in Cellma?
    A) This is not applicable
    B) Click on your conversation > click on the camera icon > find image > send message
    C) On your cellular device > Click on your conversation > click on the camera icon > find image > send message
    D) Any of the above
  9. How do you search for text/emails that were sent?
    A) This is not applicable
    B) Click on the chats icon on your left > click on Text/emails sent > Enter the relevant info in the filter boxes > click search > click show for the desired text/email
    C) On your cellular device search for sent items
    D) Go to admin > go to text/emails > click on Text/emails sent > Enter the relevant info in the filter boxes > click search > click show for the desired text/email
  10. How do you view user conversations?
    A) Click on the chat icons > click on user conversation > Enter username > click search
    B) This is not applicable
    C) Go to admin > go to user > search user > search conversation > select conversation
    D) On your cellular device > go to user > search user > search conversation > select conversation
