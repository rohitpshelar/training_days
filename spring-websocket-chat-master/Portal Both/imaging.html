Imaging

The Imaging module allows healthcare professionals to efficiently manage all imaging requests, results and reports across the organisation. Cellma is DICOM (Digital Imaging & Communications in Medicine) compliant and can integrate with imaging and diagnostic systems including PACS (Picture Archiving and Communications Systems). Images are transported directly into the patient's electronic record, reducing administration and enabling remote reporting on images.


Imaging

The Imaging module allows healthcare professionals to efficiently manage all imaging requests, results and reports across the organisation.     A) Click on the blue bar at the top> click on the patient summary icon > go to investigations > click +investigation icon
