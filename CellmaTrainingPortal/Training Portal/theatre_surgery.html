Theatre & Surgery

Cellma's Theatre & Surgery module gives surgical teams the facilities to effectively manage patients through the surgical pathway from emergency, trauma and elective surgery to post-operative care.

How to use Theatre & Surgery module

You can learn "How to use Theatre & Surgery module" here.

Workflow for Theatre & Surgery
 

Login with username and password
Click on Menu bar and select Find patient option
Search and select Patient
Confirm Patient's existing details, select Contact type and go to Patient Summary
Go Procedures section from left pane, click on Add Procedure icon. Either select procedure from the drop down and click on Add selection or search button. Or search and select snomed for the procedure from the below box based on the Patient's Medical record (Make sure that you're adding or updating the procedure to get below flow properly)
After clicking on Add Theatre icon, it will ask "Would you like to add a new referral" , click on OK option to continue
Add the procedure details and click on Add Theatres icon and fill in all details of the procedure in the form and then Save the details
Select Theatre module from the Module section on the top bar on Patient Summary page
Go to Waiting list section and Search your patient added to the waiting list
Click on Book hyperlink to book theatre slot for the Patient
Select Theatre type, theatre location, available time slot and fill in the details about the Theatre slot for the Patient in that service and then Save
Take communication consent from the Patient and Save it
It will redirect to Pending admission page, click on Add to Ward link to admit patient in ward
Select Ward,select bed, add admission details of patient and save it
Go to index page and select Theatre module again
Go through a Recovery Nursing Assessment and answer all the section questions
End the assessment and Save it
Click on Cellma to go to home page and then Logout
