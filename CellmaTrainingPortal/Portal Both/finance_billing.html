Finance billing & insurance

Cellma's Finance, Billing and Insurance module provides patient level costing information. It can generate bills and collect payments for each patient episode, thus allowing for accurate reporting and control of costs.

Finance billing & insurance

Cellma's Finance, Billing and Insurance module provides patient level costing information. It can generate bills and collect payments for each patient episode, thus allowing for accurate reporting and control of costs.

How to use Finance billing & insurance module

You can learn "How to use Finance billing & insurance module" here.

Workflow for Finance billing & insurance
 

Login with username and password
Select the Finance Module
Add amount in cashdrawer
Search and Select Patient
Generate an invoice for patient
If required add copayer(s) and/or discounts to the invoice
Once bill is generated send the bill
Click on Pay
Add amount tendered
Submit the payment either with or without receipt
Click on Refund
Enter the amount to be refunded
Exit the finance module and log out
