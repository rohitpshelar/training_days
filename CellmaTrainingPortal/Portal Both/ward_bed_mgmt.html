Cellma's Ward & Bed Management module allows ward staff to manage all aspects of ward administration using specially designed user friendly graphic interfaces.

How to use Ward and Bed Management Module

You can learn "How to use Ward and Bed Management module" here.

Workflow for Ward and Bed Management
 

Login with username and password
Select ward module from home screen
Search and select patient
Go to ward summary page through sidebar link
Add patient in ward by selecting ward from list
Book a bed by selecting bed
Add diagnosis to patient by add dignosis icon
Refresh page and Book a bed for patient
Transfer patient in different ward by selecting same bed
Select different ward and bed to transfer
Book a bed in that ward
Set ward duties by selecting set duties option on sidebar
Click on set duties icon
Select the date and set duties for each HP
Answer assessment from ward sidebar
Logout

Questions

  1. How do you find a patient from the wards module?
    A) On the left panel under Pt Ward search > select find patient
    B) Click the add patient icon at the top of the screen
    C) Click on the patient icon > Search and select patient
    D) Click on the bed > click on find patient > click save
  2. What are the steps to admitting a patient to a bed?
    A) After searching the patient > go to patient summary > search bed number > select desired bed
    B) After selecting the patient select the bed icon at the top > select admit patient and accept referral button
    C) On the service wards page after selecting a patient > click on the patient admit hyperlink > complete the admission form > select the bed
    D) All of the above are correct
  3. How do you know if a bed is occupied?
    A) The bed is grey
    B) The bed icon is no longer there
    C) The bed icon shows a red dot at the right top
    D) A red x shows across the bed
  4. Which of the following can be done by clicking on the bed icon while occupied?
    A) The patient can be discharge, transferred from one ward to another, other details can be changed
    B) The patient can only be discharges
    C) The bed is un-clickable
    D) You can on view a summary of the patient
  5. How do you allocate staff to a particular ward?
    A) This is not applicable
    B) From the service wards screen > click on duty hyperlink for the desired ward > click show ward personnel > add personnel
    C) Click the +personnel icon at the top of the service wards screen
    D) Click on the edit ward hyperlink
  6. What is mandatory to discharge a patient?
    A) Medication
    B) Appointment
    C) Discharge diagnosis
    D) All of the above
  7. What are the steps to transfer to another ward?
    A) Click on the bed the patient is placed on > Select the ward in the field that says transfer to ward > fill out all other relevant info > click the transfer to different ward button
    B) On the service ward page > select the ward > click +patient > Select the ward > Click discharge
    C) Discharge the patient from the current admitted ward > select the ward to be transferred > click admit patient hyperlink
    D) Any of the above is correct
  8. On the admitted patient list on the service ward page, how do you know how long a patient stayed on a bed?
    A) The patient name is grey
    B) The name is pink
    C) The name is blue
    D) The number of days are in brackets next to the name
  9. On the admitted patient list on the service ward page, how do you know the gender of the patient?
    A) The patient name is grey
    B) The name is pink
    C) The name is blue
    D) Females are pink males are blue
  10. How do you add vitals to a patient from the service ward page?
    A) Click on the grid view icon at the top > Click on the patient name > click the +vitals at the top > enter info > click save
    B) Click on the blue bar > click +vitals
    C) Vitals cannot be added from the service ward page
    D) Click on the patient bed > click the add vitals button > enter info > click save
