Patient Administration and Scheduling

Cellma's PAS module provides the foundation for all modules. It manages and digitally records all processes related to Registration, Admission, Discharge and Transfer (RADT) transactions of patients in Cellma

Cellma’s PAS module facilitates hassle free transfers, automatically updating the patient’s details (Including any special requirements) to the new service. The PAS module provides patient safety throughout a service by ensuring a one patient, one record system allowing the correct patient record to be accessed every time, lessening the administrative burden.

Workflow for Patient Administration and Scheduling
 

Login with username and password
Search for patient
Click on add patient button
Fill the details
Click on duplicate check button
Click on create patient
Add patient wizard
Add a referral and book an appointment
Add or edit patient insurance
Select Date,Clinic Type,Service Location,Health Professional and Time
Book Appointment and Save
Take communication consent and Save
Logout

 

Questions

  1. What are the steps to search a patient?
    A) Fill out all Patient Demographic details on Patient List page. Click the Search button shows details of patient searched
    B) Search using the patient Cellma barcode
    C) Search using first name and surname
    D) Any of the above is applicable
  2. What are the steps in registering a new patient?
    A) Search the patient > Click on the add patient button > Enter the required info and perform a duplicate check > Click on the create patient button below the duplicate check button
    B) Click on the add patient button > enter the required info >click on create patient
    C) Scan the patient’s insurance card and the profile would automatically be created
    D) All of the above
  3. What info is required on the “Add a patient wizard” screen?
    A) Name, Date of birth, Religion
    B) Insurance ID, Name
    C) Current Gender, Ethnicity, Religion, Name and Date of Birth (as taken from previous screen)
    D) Gender, Hospital reference number, Name
  4. What are the steps of adding an insurer from the patient details page?
    A) Click on the insurer icon on the patient details page > Click on +insurer on the patient insurance page > Add insurer/ fill out relevant information and click save
    B) Enter this on the add patient wizard screen
    C) Click the PIP icon on the patient details page > fill out the relevant info
    D) Go to patient summary > click on view > click on add insurer > fill out relevant info
  5. What are the steps to add a Next of Kin to a patient profile?
    A) Merge the two patient profile using the first name and surname
    B) This is not applicable in Cellma
    C) Click on the PIP icon on the patients details page > Enter all the relevant info of the PIP > Click save > Cellma will confirm this has been linked
    D) Click on the duplicate patient button > enter the barcode of the PIP patient > Cellma confirms this is linked
  6. How do you add an appointment for a patient from the appointment module?
    A) Go to the patient demographic page > click on the +app button > type relevant info > click save
    B) Go to patient summary > Click on view > Click on find appointment > fill out relevant info > click save
    C) Go to the appointment module > search and select the patient >Select the desired clinic type, service location and HP > fill out the relevant info > click save and show patient or save
    D) Any of the above is applicable
  7. Which of the following are the steps to adding a referral from the patient module?
    A) On the patient demographic page, click “Add Referral” icon > Select Service Location > Source of Referral > Referral Reason > Referral Accepted (confirmed) by selecting “Accept Referral” radio button
    B) Once any data have been entered under any service, the referral would automatically be accepted
    C) The doctor must approve this by doing an assessment
    D) All of the above
  8. Which of the following are the steps to book an appointment from adding a referral?
    A) With a patient selected, Select *Appointment Type (New, Review, Emergency or Walk In) > Appointment Reason (if applicable) > Clicks on Save - Proceeds to Communication Consent page
    B) Select “Accept Referral & Schedule Appointment” radio button on ADD A REFERRAL page
    C) From the appointment bookings page, select the “Accept Referral” radio button
    D) Go to the patient summary and click the referral tab on the left panel
  9. What are the steps to generating a letter for a patient?
    A) On the left panel on the patient summary screen, select the letters tab and click the print hyperlink
    B) Scan the patient barcode > the letter comes up in a pop up screen > click the print button
    C) Search and select the patient > got to patient summary > Click on view or Letters and summary > select the desired letter > click save
    D) Any of the above methods can be used
  10. How do you edit a patient profile?
    A) A patient cannot be edited
    B) Go to the admin module > click on edit patient profile > scan patient barcode > click on edit
    C) Search and select the patient > go to patient demographics page > click on the edit hyperlink
    D) Search and select patient > go to the contact history page > click view > click edit
